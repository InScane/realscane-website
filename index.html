About Me

My name is Michael West, better known as my online Social Media pseudonym InScane. I am an American actor, Talk Show Host, and YouTuber. I am mostly popular for my Roblox and Minecraft Let's Plays and gaming videos as well as live streams. I first started uploading videos back on March 29, 2018 on my YouTube channel. I wanted to make a difference within the Social Media Community as in raise money for the people that need it like family and children hospitals and helping other creators grow their channels and gain the attention they need. Throughout my career and all the content being produced, I had many burnouts where I just didn't upload for weeks or took many weeks to relief stress but overcame the burnouts by just doing what I love, and getting rest before and after the content creation to freshen up.
